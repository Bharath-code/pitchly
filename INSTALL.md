# Installing Pitchly (Beta)

## Step 1 — Download

Download the ZIP from the link sent to you and unzip it somewhere easy to find (e.g., your Desktop).

## Step 2 — Open Chrome Extensions

Open Chrome and go to: **chrome://extensions**

Or: Menu → More tools → Extensions

## Step 3 — Enable Developer Mode

Toggle **Developer mode** ON in the top-right corner.

## Step 4 — Load the Extension

1. Click **Load unpacked**
2. Navigate to the unzipped folder
3. Select the `extension/` subfolder (not the outer folder)
4. Click **Select**

Pitchly icon should now appear in your Chrome toolbar.

## Step 5 — Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome toolbar → find Pitchly → click the pin icon.

## Step 6 — Join Your Call

> **Important:** For full both-sides transcription, join your Zoom calls via the **browser** (zoom.us), not the Zoom desktop app.
>
> Google Meet works automatically — no changes needed.

## Step 7 — Start Listening

1. Join a Google Meet or Zoom Web call
2. Click the Pitchly icon in your toolbar
3. Click **Start Listening**
4. Keep the extension popup visible until you see the audio mode badge turn green ("Tab + Mic")

## That's It

When your prospect raises an objection, a response card will slide in from the bottom-right of your screen within ~3 seconds.

- The card will **auto-dismiss after 15 seconds**
- Click **×** to dismiss it immediately
- Press **Escape** to dismiss it with the keyboard
